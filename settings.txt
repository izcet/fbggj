Urban
density: moderate
terrain: patterned (grid or regular geometric shapes)
lighting: regular/irregular. streetlights and car headlights

Forest
density: low
terrain: random (trees in a sparse or dense pattern, rocks and other obstacles)
lighting: low. maybe an occasional campfire, potentially moonlight?

Caves
Density: dense
terrain: labrynthine caverns, winding and looping tunnels
lighting: rare. occasional bioluminescent mushrooms.

Indoors
density: dense
terrain: patterned (grid of rooms with doors and hallways)
lighting: variable. lamps and lights in rooms, but the lights can be switched on or off
